About Us

Creative force behind our projects is our event designer Ritu Agarwal

Her path to event design started with Bachelor of Arts (1992), Loreto House, Kolkata,India followed by Diploma in Interior Designing (1993), Jenson and Nicholson and various Diplomas in Painting, Sculpting, Flower Arrangement, Floor Decoration.

When she switched to Event Design , she never looked back.

Her academic background and rich experience ensures that your Event Decor will be tasteful, creative and cost-effective. Providing party and wedding decorations for over 12 months we specialize in creating stunning and unique decorations to transform your venue, helping to create the event of your dreams.

As party and wedding decorators, we offer our services for weddings, corporate events, diwali, ring ceremony, baby shower, anniversaries and birthdays.We show you designs we believe should suit you, taking your budget into consideration.No matter what kind of event decoration you need, we have the talent, skills, experience and inventory to make your next event spectacular. We offer the very best in party décor and we can put together the perfect look for you.We give that special finishing touch that says a million words in your pictures and stays with your memory.

Ms. Ritu Agarwal has been honored with the STAR Regional Awards - Female Performer category in Runner's up position(BNI Annual Meet). The event was hosted at JW Marriott on 18th August 2017.
