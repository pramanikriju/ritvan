About Us

Creative force behind our projects is our event designer Ritu Agarwal. Perfection is brainchild of our Founder.

Her path to event design started with Bachelor of Arts (1992), Loreto House, Kolkata,India followed by Diploma in Interior Designing (1993), Jenson and Nicholson and various Diplomas in Painting, Sculpting, Flower Arrangement, Floor Decoration.

When she switched to Event Design , she knew it requires- Foresight, follow through, attention to detail & unravelling exactly as planned.

Her academic background and rich experience ensures that your Event Decor will be tasteful, creative and cost-effective. Providing party and wedding decorations for over 12 months we specialize in creating stunning and unique decorations to transform your venue, helping to create the event of your dreams.
