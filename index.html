Our Story

Creative force behind our projects is our event designer Ritu Agarwal.
Her path to event design started with Bachelor of Arts (1992), Loreto House, Kolkata, India followed by Diploma in Interior Designing (1993), Jenson & Nicholson & various Diplomas in Painting, Sculpting, Flower Arrangement & Floor Decoration.
Once she switched to Event Design, there was no looking back!
Her academic background and rich experience ensure that your Events will be tasteful, creative & cost-effective. Providing party & wedding decorations for over 6 years, we specialise in creating stunning and unique decorations to transform your venue, helping to create the event of your dreams.
Ms. Ritu Agarwal has been honoured with the STAR Regional Awards - Female Performer category in Runner's up position (BNI Annual Meet) hosted at JW Marriott on 18th August 2017.

Corporate Social Responsibility

  • Ritu Agarwal has been actively involved in women empowerment in association with Hamari Muskaan, an NGO working to help women learn skills to become financially independent.

  • She's also an active member of the Friends of Tribal Society (FTS), helping in the eduction and development of rural children.
