Our Story

Creative force behind our projects is our event designer Ritu Agarwal
Her path to event design started with Bachelor of Arts (1992), Loreto House, Kolkata, India followed by Diploma in Interior Designing (1993), Jenson & Nicholson and various Diplomas in Painting, Sculpting, Flower Arrangement & Floor Decoration.
Once she switched to Event Design, there was no looking back!
Her academic background and rich experience ensure that your Events will be tasteful, creative & cost-effective. Providing party & wedding decorations for over 6 years, we specialize in creating stunning and unique decorations to transform your venue, helping to create the event of your dreams.
As party and wedding planners & organisers, we offer our services for every possible event you can think of, no matter how big or small. We show you prototypes we believe should suit you, taking your budget into consideration. No matter what kind of event decoration you need, we have the talent, skills, experience and inventory to make your next event spectacular. We offer the very best in party planning and we can put together the perfect look for you also adding special finishing touch that says a million words in your pictures and stays with you, in your memory.
Ms. Ritu Agarwal has been honoured with the STAR Regional Awards - Female Performer category in Runner's up position (BNI Annual Meet) hosted at JW Marriott on 18th August 2017.
